Today's foundation is the bridge between skin care and cosmetics. Whether in cream, powder or liquid form, foundation should go on smoothly and evenly without adding shine or changing your skin's color. It should effortlessly hide imperfections, smooth out fine lines, even out skin tone and give a natural finish to your skin. Here at Dermstore, we offer an array of foundations often formulated with sunscreen, alpha hydroxy acids, moisturizers and other anti-aging ingredients.
